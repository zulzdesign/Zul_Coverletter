APPLICATION FOR VISUALISER ROLE

Dear STF Team,

I’m writing to express my keen interest in contributing to STF as both a Visualiser and a Programmer. With a background as an Architectural Designer and a recent transition into Software Development, I bring a unique combination of spatial intelligence and technical fluency- an ideal match for a team working at the intersection of AI reshaping the Architecture industry.

Your mission to reprogram architecture really resonates with me. Over the past few years, many industries have rapidly embraced AI, but architecture has been slower to adapt-some practices are still hesitant to change. Many still don't understand that AI isn't meant to replace architects, but to enhance workflows by streamlining the repetitive parts of the architectural process. That’s exactly what I’m passionate about.

Given STF’s focus on AI innovation, I offer my skills not only as a visualiser but also as a programmer-a team member fluent in both physical and digital structure. I’m confident I can contribute to both the creative and technical layers of your work, helping turn your ideas into functional prototypes.

I’d love the opportunity to speak with you about how I can contribute to STF. Thank you for considering my application, I look forward to the possibility of working with a team that’s pushing the boundaries of the architecture world.

Kind regards,

Zulhilmi Zambry
